About Jugal School

Established in 1994 A.D. (2051 B.S.), Jugal School is a private co-educational institution located in Dhapakhel-23, Lalitpur, Nepal. We are committed to nurturing young minds through quality education and holistic development that balances academic excellence with character formation.

Our institution is recognized for its commitment to innovation, cultural values, and student-centric learning methodologies. With our motto "Do Good, Be Good", we believe in creating an environment where every student can discover their potential and become responsible global citizens.

Our Mission

To provide quality education rooted in our motto "Do Good, Be Good" that develops intellectual capability, moral character, and social responsibility in our students.

Our Vision

To be recognized as a leading educational institution in Lalitpur that produces responsible, ethical, and socially-conscious individuals.

Principal's Message

"At Jugal School, we believe that education goes beyond academics. We strive to nurture good character alongside intellectual development. Our motto 'Do Good, Be Good' guides every aspect of our educational philosophy. We welcome each student with commitment to their holistic growth and well-being."

— Principal, Jugal School

Admission Process

1
Application Form

Submit the completed application form with supporting documents online or in person.

2
Assessment

Qualifying students undergo entrance assessment in English, Mathematics, and Reasoning.

3
Interview

Shortlisted candidates and parents participate in a personal interview with our admission team.

4
Selection Result

Merit-based selection results are communicated to families with enrollment confirmation details.

Requirements

  • Birth Certificate (attested copy)
  • Previous Academic Record / Transfer Certificate
  • Medical Certificate (updated immunization)
  • Two Passport-sized Photographs
  • Parent's Identification and Residence Proof
  • Bank Statement / Income Proof (for fee assessment)

Additional documents may be requested if required for specific admissions.
