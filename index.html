About Jugal School

A private co-educational institution in Dhapakhel-23, Lalitpur — shaping futures since 1994.

Established in 1994 A.D. (2051 B.S.), Jugal School is committed to nurturing young minds through quality education and holistic development that balances academic excellence with character formation.

Our institution is recognized for its commitment to innovation, cultural values, and student-centric learning methodologies. With our motto "Do Good, Be Good", we believe in creating an environment where every student can discover their potential and become responsible global citizens.

🎯

Our Mission

To provide quality education that develops intellectual capability, moral character, and social responsibility.

🌟

Our Vision

To be a leading institution in Lalitpur that produces responsible, ethical, and socially-conscious individuals.

Principal's Message

"At Jugal School, we believe that education goes beyond academics. We strive to nurture good character alongside intellectual development. Our motto 'Do Good, Be Good' guides every aspect of our educational philosophy. We welcome each student with commitment to their holistic growth and well-being."

— Principal, Jugal School

Academic Programs

Comprehensive curriculum designed for holistic development

🎨

Playgroup & Pre-school

Early childhood development through play-based learning. Focus on social, emotional, and cognitive development.

  • Language exposure (English & Nepali)
  • Basic numeracy concepts
  • Creative play & activities
📚

Basic Level (Grades 1-8)

Comprehensive foundational education with emphasis on core academics and character development.

  • English, Nepali, Mathematics, Science
  • Social Studies & Values Education
  • Computer Science, Arts & Sports
🎓

Secondary (Grades 9-10)

Advanced learning preparing students for national board examinations with emphasis on analytical skills.

  • Science & Social Stream options
  • Mathematics, English, Nepali
  • Board exam preparation (SEE)
